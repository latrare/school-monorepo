\begin{abstract}
  This article discusses the progress of the ``Quantum Internet'' in the
  context of the fundamental knowledge required to understand quantum computing.
  The growing feasibility of the Quantum Internet represents the formation of a
  new frontier of computer networking research. This review hopes to imbue the
  reader with an appreciation for this new frontier in computer networking after
  providing the reader with enough foundational knowledge so they can appreciate
  the progress detailed in this article.
\end{abstract}
